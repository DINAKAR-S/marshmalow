Our originator, settled on the choice to close on Sundays in 2010 when he opened his first eatery in Chennai,Tamilnadu. Having worked seven days in eateries open 24 hours, Truett saw the significance of shutting on Sundays with the goal that he and his representatives could set aside one day to rest and love whether they pick – a training we maintain today.
